# Martinsburg Seed Event Research

Date: 2026-07-09

Default pilot location: Martinsburg, WV
Default reach: 50 miles
Maximum user reach: 150 miles

These listings are prototype examples only. They are publicly sourced opportunities or community events that appear aligned with the Handprint "Do Something" rubric, but they are not official Handprint partners and should not issue official rewards until a World Enabler registers, confirms the role, and accepts post-event verification.

## Seed Examples

### Berkeley County Meals on Wheels

Source: https://www.berkeleycountymealsonwheels.com/

Why it fits: Direct delivery of meals and wellness checks to homebound and disabled residents. The organization reports 61,342 meals served in 2025 and provides a named Martinsburg location.

Handprint status: Strong action candidate. Needs World Enabler route confirmation and safety/privacy guidelines before official rewards.

### CCAP/Loaves & Fishes

Source: https://ccaploavesandfishes.com/

Why it fits: Emergency food and financial assistance for low-income and underserved Berkeley County residents, including food donations, food drives, and volunteer roles.

Handprint status: Strong action candidate. Needs shift confirmation and privacy-safe reporting.

### Salvation Army Martinsburg Corps

Source: https://martinsburg.salvationarmypotomac.org/martinsburg/volunteer-1/

Why it fits: Publicly lists volunteer roles including food pantry, disaster services, youth and teen programs, grounds work, office help, and seasonal service.

Handprint status: Strong action candidate for concrete roles. Youth/disaster roles need extra safeguards.

### United Way of the Eastern Panhandle volunteerEP

Source: https://uwayep.org/volunteer

Why it fits: Connects volunteers with nonprofit, school, and government opportunities across Morgan, Berkeley, and Jefferson Counties.

Handprint status: Good discovery partner candidate. Rewards should attach to the selected host-agency project, not simply browsing or signing up.

### Berkeley County Youth Fair

Source: https://www.berkeleycountyyouthfair.org/

Why it fits: A dated August 1-8, 2026 Martinsburg event with 350+ youth exhibitors and volunteer participation paths.

Handprint status: Good community support candidate, but youth-facing roles should stay reward-locked until safeguards and role definitions are confirmed.

### Habitat for Humanity Eastern Panhandle ReStore

Source: https://www.habitat.org/local/restore?zip=25411

Why it fits: Habitat lists an Eastern Panhandle ReStore at 1353 Edwin Miller Blvd., Martinsburg, WV. ReStore volunteering can support reuse, housing, and repair work when roles are clearly defined.

Handprint status: Good action candidate for donation processing, logistics, repair support, or housing-support shifts. Shopping/donating alone should not earn points.
